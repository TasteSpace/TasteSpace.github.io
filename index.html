About Us

Taste Space is a small group that loves food and loves the internet just as much. We stumble across new viral hacks and trends, and instead of just watching them, we actually try them out. Sometimes they work, sometimes they fail, but either way, it's always fun (and usually most of them are delicious).

Taste Space is our fun zone where we share our experience with you. We taste and react honestly so you'll know which trends are worth trying at home.
